    Snapshot

    Where I've been

    I completed my BSE in Mechanical Engineering (ME) from the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor in 2021. My passion in ME was in control systems/automation and human-centered design. However, as I completed my degree, I realized that I enjoyed the programming challenges that my courses brought a bit more than the engineering coursework. Luckily, while I was there, I took classes in computer science (EECS 183 and EECS 280) and in intelligent systems (CEE 575) which fueled my interest in interdisciplinary projects. Specifically, I want to develop more projects incorporating both hardware and software to learn new tools and concepts.

    What I'm Doing

    I am currently an Entry Level Software Developer (IT-OT) at General Motors.

    What the Future Holds

    With my interest in interdisciplinary projects, I hope to be a technical leader in an impactful organization. Ultimately, I want to continue learning and growing while encouraging others do the same!
